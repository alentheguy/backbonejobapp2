About the company

Backbone's vision is to become the platform for core gaming on mobile devices, allowing anyone to access and play games anywhere.

Similar to Roku, Backbone is a universal platform for game streaming and, more broadly, core gaming on mobile. Players use it to enjoy App Store games, Xbox Game Pass, PlayStation Remote Play and more through compelling gameplay, content creation, and community.

It's backed by Index, the same investor behind Discord, Roblox, and Rec Room, as well as companies like Figma and Robinhood.
