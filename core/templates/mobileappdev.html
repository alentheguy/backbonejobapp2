We're seeking a talented Mobile App Developer to join our team. In this role, you'll be responsible for developing and maintaining high-quality mobile applications that offer seamless user experiences. What you'll be doing

  • Develop and maintain mobile applications for iOS and/or Android platforms

  • Ensure the performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications. Identify and troubleshoot issues, bugs, and performance bottlenecks

What's required

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience
  • Proven experience developing and launching mobile applications for iOS and/or Android platforms

  • Strong understanding of mobile app architecture, design patterns, and best practices
  • Experience with RESTful APIs and integration of third-party libraries and services
  • Solid understanding of mobile UI/UX principles and ability to implement responsive designs
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills, with a keen attention to detail
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills

  • Experience with cross-platform development frameworks such as React Native or Xamarin
  • Familiarity with cloud-based services and server-side technologies
  • Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and automated testing frameworks

  • Passion for mobile technology and eagerness to stay updated on the latest trends and advancements
