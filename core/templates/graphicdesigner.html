We are seeking a talented Graphic Designer to join our creative team. As a Graphic Designer, you will be responsible for creating visually stunning designs that effectively communicate our brand message across various digital and print channels. This role offers the opportunity to work remotely or on-site, depending on preference and location.

What you'll be doing

  • Create engaging and innovative designs for digital and print media, including social media graphics, website assets, marketing collateral, presentations, and more
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand project requirements and develop creative concepts that align with brand guidelines and objectives
  • Translate conceptual ideas into visually appealing layouts and designs with attention to detail and creativity
  • Use industry-standard design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) to execute design projects
  • Stay updated on design trends, techniques, and best practices to continuously improve design quality and effectiveness
  • Ensure all designs meet brand standards, are visually consistent, and resonate with target audiences

What's required

  • Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Visual Communication, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience
  • Proven experience as a Graphic Designer, with a strong portfolio showcasing a range of design projects across digital and print media
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) and other relevant design tools
  • Solid understanding of design principles, typography, color theory, and composition
  • Excellent creative and conceptual thinking skills, with the ability to translate ideas into compelling visual designs
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously under tight deadlines
  • Effective communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to present and articulate design concepts to stakeholders

What's ideal

  • Experience with motion graphics and video editing software (e.g., Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro)
  • Familiarity with web design principles and tools (e.g., Adobe XD, Sketch, Figma)
  • Knowledge of print production processes and requirements
  • Experience working in a fast-paced environment or agency setting
  • Passion for design and a proactive approach to learning and self-improvement
