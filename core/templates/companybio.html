Backbone

About

Backbone offers a mobile gaming controller that is cloud-gaming and remote-play compatible. It is a low-power, low-latency gaming device that works with the iPhone and does not require charging, allowing for responsive gaming anywhere. Backbone allows gamers to simply snap their phone into the Backbone One controller, launch the Backbone App, and begin playing mobile, console, or PC games from any location.

Mission

Backbone's core mission is centered around revolutionizing the product development process for the fashion and consumer goods industries. By providing a powerful, user-friendly platform, Backbone aims to streamline collaboration, enhance efficiency, and enable teams to bring their creative visions to life with ease. Their commitment to innovation and continuous improvement drives their pursuit of simplifying complex workflows, ultimately empowering brands to create exceptional products and thrive in a competitive market.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Backbone thrives on pushing the boundaries of technology, inspiring employees to develop groundbreaking solutions and drive the evolution of the digital landscape.
  • Collaboration: Emphasizing a strong sense of teamwork, Backbone fosters an environment where diverse ideas are welcomed, and cross-functional collaboration is key to achieving success.
  • Efficiency: Backbone is dedicated to streamlining processes and optimizing performance, ensuring that both its products and its team members operate at the highest level of efficiency.
  • Customer-Centricity: At the core of Backbone's values is a commitment to understanding and addressing the unique needs of its clients, delivering exceptional experiences and tailored solutions.
  • Growth and development: Backbone invests in the personal and professional growth of its employees, offering ample opportunities for skill enhancement, learning, and career progression.
