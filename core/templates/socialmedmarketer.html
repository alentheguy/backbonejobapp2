We are looking for a talented Social Media Marketer to join our marketing team. As a Social Media Marketer, you will be responsible for developing and implementing social media strategies to grow our brand presence, engage our audience, and drive traffic and conversions. This role offers the opportunity to work remotely or on-site, depending on preference and location.

What you'll be doing

  • Develop and execute social media strategies to increase brand awareness, reach, and engagement across various social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok)
  • Create compelling content for social media posts, including graphics, videos, and written copy, that align with brand voice and messaging
  • Manage social media accounts, including scheduling and publishing content, monitoring comments and messages, and responding to inquiries and feedback in a timely manner
  • Analyze social media performance metrics and insights to track progress, identify trends, and optimize strategies for maximum impact and ROI
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including marketing, content, design, and sales, to align social media efforts with overall marketing objectives and campaigns
  • Stay updated on social media trends, algorithm changes, and best practices to continuously improve social media strategies and tactics

What's required

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience
  • Proven experience as a Social Media Marketer, with a strong portfolio showcasing successful social media campaigns and results
  • Deep understanding of social media platforms, algorithms, and best practices for organic and paid social media marketing
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to create engaging content and communicate effectively with diverse audiences
  • Strong analytical skills and ability to interpret data and metrics to drive informed decisions and optimizations
  • Creative thinking and problem-solving skills, with a knack for generating innovative ideas and strategies
  • Ability to multitask, prioritize tasks, and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
  • Proficiency in social media management tools and analytics platforms

What's ideal

  • Experience with social media advertising platforms (e.g., Facebook Ads Manager, Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads)
  • Knowledge of SEO principles and content optimization techniques
  • Familiarity with graphic design tools (e.g., Adobe Creative Suite) for creating social media assets
  • Previous experience working in a B2B or B2C marketing role
  • Passion for social media and staying updated on industry trends and developments
